Privacy Policy

This document explains how personal information is collected, disclosed and used by Nixter with respect to your use of Nixter’s site (www.nixter.com), Nixter’s mobile App and other networking services related. This information might be modified, but we will notify you for any changes that we made indicating the day it was last updated.

Personal info?

"Personal info" means information that identifies an individual(such as name, age, gender, relationship status, email address, telephone number, among others). Personal info does not include the data we collect about the use of our site and our services. Our privacy policy does not restrict our collection and use of this information.

What personal info do we collect?

We actively collect certain information you voluntarily provide to us, such as when you create an account and profile, send us an email or post information or other content to our site or App. We automatically collect certain information when you use our our services, such as your IP address, your operating system, the browser type, the address of a referring site and your activity on our site or mobile app like check-ins, posts and location. We treat this information as personal information if we combine it with or link it to any of the identifying information mentioned above.We may also automatically collect certain information through the use of "cookies" or web beacons.

Use of collected info?

We use personal information collected through our site and our services for the purposes described in our privacy policy or elsewhere on our site. For example, we may use personal information we collect:
  • - to provide our services or information you request, and to process and complete any transactions;
  • - respond to your emails, submissions, questions, comments, requests, and complaints and provide customer service;
  • - to monitor and analyze site usage and trends, and to personalize and improve our site and our users’ experiences on our site, such as providing ads, content, or features that match their profiles or interests, and to increase our site's functionality and user friendliness;
  • -to send you confirmations, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages and otherwise facilitate your use of, and our administration and operation of, our site; and
  • -or any other purpose for which the information was collected.

Sharing your info?!?! OMG!

We will not share your personal information through our site or our services with third parties. We only share statistic information and different "customer targeting" tools to Nightclubs working directly with Nixter.

Personal privacy settings

You can set up your own privacy settings on your profile to enable or disable being checked in by friends in parties, bars or others with Nixter's mobile app.

We work every day harder to have the best security tools to protect your personal info.
